## Terms and Conditions
1.  **Retainer:** A standard retainer of $3,000.00 is required prior to the commencement of technical review or document analysis. Fees will be billed against the retainer.
2.  **Billing Increment:** Hourly services are billed in 15-minute increments.
3.  **Expenses:** Reimbursable expenses include airfare (Economy/Premium), lodging, ground transportation, and a standard per-diem for meals.
4.  **Cancellation:** Cancellations for depositions or trial testimony made less than 72 hours in advance are subject to the minimum daily/hourly fee.
