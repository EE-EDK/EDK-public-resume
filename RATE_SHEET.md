## Terms and Conditions
1.  **Retainer:** A standard retainer of $3,000.00 is required prior to commencement of technical review or document analysis. All fees are billed against the retainer; additional invoicing occurs upon retainer depletion.
2.  **Billing Increment:** All hourly services are billed in 15-minute increments.
3.  **Reimbursable Expenses:** Airfare (Economy or Premium Economy), lodging, ground transportation, and a standard per-diem for meals. Specialized testing equipment or third-party laboratory fees billed at cost with prior approval.
4.  **Cancellation Policy:** Cancellations for depositions or trial testimony made less than 72 hours in advance are subject to the applicable minimum daily or hourly fee.

## Forensic Analysis Capabilities
Mr. Kunz maintains a fully-equipped professional laboratory for forensic examination of electronic hardware, enabling independent physical evidence examination without reliance on third-party laboratories. Capabilities include: Saleae Pro 16 multi-channel logic analyzers, Metcal ultrasonic soldering and hot-flow rework stations, 4K digital microscope with precision manipulator, and high-precision bench instrumentation. This facility supports failure mode analysis, counterfeit component detection, workmanship assessment per IPC-A-610, and electrical fault sequence reconstruction.
